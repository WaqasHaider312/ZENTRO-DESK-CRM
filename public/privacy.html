Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Zentro Desk ("we", "our", or "us") operates a customer support CRM platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Organization details (name, website)
  • Messages and conversations managed through our platform
  • Facebook and Instagram Page access tokens when you connect your pages

2. How We Use Facebook and Instagram Data

When you connect a Facebook Page or Instagram account to Zentro Desk, we:

  • Access your Page's messages solely to display them in your support inbox
  • Send messages on your behalf only when you explicitly reply through our platform
  • Store page access tokens securely to maintain the connection
  • Never sell or share your Facebook/Instagram data with third parties
  • Only request permissions necessary for customer support functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Access tokens are encrypted at rest.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with Meta (Facebook/Instagram) platforms under their Platform Terms. We comply with Meta's Platform Policy regarding data use and storage.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, email us or visit zentro-desk-crm.vercel.app/data-deletion. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Contact

For privacy questions: support@zentro.app
